Professional

A software engineer originally from Italy and now working in London, UK, Stefano Ricciardi has started his career developing mobile applications for Motorola. Around a decade ago he's turned to enterprise applications development and he's now currently working for Opentable.

Hobbies

He loves running and hitting the gym. He also a passion for photography, both digital and traditional black and white (which he insists on developing and printing on his own). His favorite subjects are his beloved wife and their two kids.
